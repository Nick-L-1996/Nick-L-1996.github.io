Major Qualifying Project - Autonomous Landmine Detection Rover

My Major Qualifying Project at WPI was to design an autonomous rover capable of detecting landmines with small amounts of metal and physically mark them on the ground so that an octocopter drone could drop a sandbag on it to detonate. The goal between this rover and the flying drone was to remove human and animal operators from the minefields, automating the entire process from searching to removal of landmines. This page will describe my contributions to the project.
